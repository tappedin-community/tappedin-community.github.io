LACOE : Welcome

LACOE is a premiere provider of integrated, educational programs and services, from birth to adulthood, in a richly diverse and multicultural global environment. LACOE provides support to teachers, administrators, parents, schools and the county's 81 school districts, which serve 1.7 million public school children. LACOE also provides direct instruction for students with disabilities, juvenile offenders and those who are at-risk.

LACOE offers several exclusive services to our TAPPED IN LACOE members. Feel free to browser our group and course offerings, and other resources available to our TAPPED IN members.
