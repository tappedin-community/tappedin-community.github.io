LACOE : Courses : STELLAR

STELLAR (Supporting Teachers of English Learners in Language Arts and Reading) is a Distributed Learning/Multimedia Project for Improving Reading Instruction for English Language Learners in Grades K-8.

STELLAR Course Description
In this seven week course, students will increase their familiarity with the goals embedded in the student content standards and refine their skills in designing and using different types of assessment at strategic points before, during, and after instruction. They will deepen their understanding of how the information provided from assessments will be critical in determining what to teach, how much the students are learning, and whether the students have achieved mastery. By learning to carefully monitor progress, they will develop and deliver curricula and instruction that will lead students to the achievement of the content standards in their subject area.

Course Credits: 2 units of Academic Credit available through CSU Dominguez Hills.
30 Professional Growth Hours.
