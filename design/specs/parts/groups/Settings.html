Settings

The group founder and moderators with editing privileges can customize the group room and manage various elements of the group.

Room Configuration enables you to change the look of the room for your group. You can also make these changes from the edit links on the Welcome page of your group. 

Group Configuration allows you to change the overall setting for the group, including keywords to idenitfy the group.

Member Management  allows you to give members certain privileges or restrictions.
