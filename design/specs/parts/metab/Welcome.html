My Office

In your office , you can hold meetings, share files and links, engage in discussions with others. Having your own office gives you your own personal space in Tapped In and another form of expression to let people know whoyou are and your interests. Think of Me as your backpack organizer and your office as the extension of that were you can actually meet with people on topics of your choice.
