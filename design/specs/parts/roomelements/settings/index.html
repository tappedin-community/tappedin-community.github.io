Settings (Specifically, Room Configuration)

The Settings link on an individual room lets the owner edit the Room Configuration. Additional settings are available on Group and Course rooms; see the Group and Course specs for information on other settings. This document will focus on Room Configuration settings.

Room Configuration settings allow control over the room, such as the name of the room, the appearance of the welcome page, passageways from the room, etc.

Specifically, the available items for edit on a room include the Room Name and Image (always visible), as well as all of the possible sticky notes visible from the Welcome page (see Welcome page for more information):

Welcome Text [1]
Announcements [2]
Quick Look [2]
Featured Items [2]
Generic Notes (up to 3) [1]
Generic Images (up to 3) [2]

If the room owner clicks the Settings link, she will see a page like the following, with all of the items available for editing listed, along with their current value, checkbox to specify if the value is visible or not, and a button to edit a value. [1] Note that the room name and image are always visible and cannot be hidden. [1]

Notes

How do we do layout of items on the Welcome page? Should it be specifiable in the Settings? We could have 2 columns for items in the Welcome page and let the user specify which column they want which items to go into [3]. Or , we could just lay them out in the order they are specified, and use some simple heuristic [1] (e.g., if X items are visible, the first X/2 go into column 1 and the remainder go in column 2). The heuristic approach may be easies, and acceptable in most cases. Future releases after this one could offer a layout manager [3]
